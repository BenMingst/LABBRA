About the LABBRA System

The LABBRA System is an innovative, advanced impact detection technology designed to protect pedestrians by reducing response times in emergency situations.

With a vision detection system powered by edge computing devices and wide-angle cameras, it helps emergency responders act quickly and efficiently, potentially saving lives. Named after UCF students who tragically lost their lives while walking on campus, the LABBRA System honors their memory by improving pedestrian safety through cutting-edge technology.

The Problem

Every year, thousands of pedestrians are killed or injured due to delayed emergency response times. The LABBRA System aims to fix this critical issue and save lives.
